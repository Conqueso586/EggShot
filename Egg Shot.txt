Egg Shot
Kelly Holstrom

Egg gets shot from car,
person gets shot out of egg on seat,
person can parashute to the goal,
Has tractor beam under seat for slight movement.

Second Character is a plane,
person ejects and has a glider with rockets,
can detach to drop

Third Character is a police van,
car blows up and police motorcycle busts out the front,
person can slide the motor cycle and wheelie.
